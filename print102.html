Printing in 102

To print a textfile, residing on eeyore, on an ECC102 laser printer:

    1. You must be on a PC rather than a Unix workstation.
    2. Double click on the My Computer icon.
    3. In the Address field at the top of the window, enter:
      1. ftp://userid@eeyore.stcloudstate.edu
    4. When the Login As window appears, enter your  eeyore userid (again) and password. 
      Ensure that the save password box has not been checked off.
    5. Locate the file you  intend to print, e.g. lab3.cpp, and drag it to the desktop.
    6. Do NOT open the copy in the My Computer window by double clicking on it -- this may result in your  eeyore account password being compromised.
    7. Close the My Computer window that is open to your eeyore files.
    8. Open the file in a PC editor such as Textpad.
    9. From within the editor, select the Print option under the File button.  When the printing dialog box appears, set the Name field to the HP LaserJet 4050 series PCL if not already selected.  Then press the OK button.
    10. Ffrom the PHAROS Popup Cient window that appears, enter a name for your output (your userid is a good choice);  then press the Next button.  In the window that appears, assign a print password to your output file so that someone else will be unable to print your output.  Push the OK button.
    11. Exit the editor.
    12. Drag the desktop copy of your file the the Recycle Bin.
    13. Right click on the Recyle Bin icon.  Select the option to Empty Recycle Bin.  In the Confirm File Delete window that apears, push the Yes button to confirm that you want to delete the file.  After a few seconds the Recycle Bin should appear empty.
    14. Go over to the print station and follow the screen prompts to receive your output.  note that a campus card with a positive balance is required to pay for your printouts.  Ensure that you press the Exit button when finished.
