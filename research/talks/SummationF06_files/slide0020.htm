Hybrid Algorithm III
•The limit of Hybrid Algorithm II is for the bins to be indexed by exponent value. There are a couple of benefits to this:
1.Same-sign operations:
[e.g. sum of squares]
1. Reinsertion is trivial : place partial sum in next higher bin.
2.Maximum error per partial sum will be < ½ulp.
2.Opposite–sign operations:
1.Reinsertion is O(1): place partial sum by index.
