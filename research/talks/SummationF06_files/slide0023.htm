Hybrid Algorithm IV
•We can accomplish this now at the expense of doubling our bins:
1.partition terms by both exponent & l.s.b.
2.if new terms enter into a partial sum, it is with another term having the same l.s.b.
3.place the partial sum in the appropriate one of the two adjacent higher bins based on its l.s.b.
•Now, all partial sums will be exact until we exhaust terms to place in bins.  Then it remains only to add together the bin contents.
