Homework Assignment 1: COMPUTERS VS CALCULATORS

The intent of this assignment is to both

  • Learn more about what differentiates a calculator from a computer, and
  • Exercise your new Unix skills.

On our csci server you will:

  1. create a hw1 directory;
  2. write a Comparison/Contrast essay consisting of
    1. the outline (with indents) in one file, and
    2. the essay (with a bibliography) in another.
    on the topic of comparing and constrasting calculators and computers using your own words and properly cited and styled quotations.
  3. use the Unix filter, spell, to ensure that your spelling is correct.
  4. use the Unix script command to generate a file containing your outline, your essay, and a demonstration that they pass the spell-checking test.
  5. use lpr to print to the ECC102 printer.
  6. Note: (ensure that both your outline and essay file include as a header your name, course and section, assignment number, and date)
