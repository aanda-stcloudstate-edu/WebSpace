Problem Motivation and Definition:

This lab is intended to provide experience with initializing, printing, and indexing of two-dimensional arrays.

We want to write a program which will initialize and print a two-dimensional array. The array will be filled with a Pascal triangle. Pascal's triangle provides a simple means of calculating a wealth of well known and useful series including binomial coefficients and Fibonacci numbers (see p. 269) as well as for counting certain combinations.
Please refer to the following references for the details:

  • Pascal's Triangle
  • Pascal's Triangle -- from MathWorld
  • Binomial Coefficient -- from MathWorld
