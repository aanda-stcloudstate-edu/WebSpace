Note: Everything in this lab assignment, unless otherwise stated, must be performed on the csci2 server.
  1. Create the directory lab4 in your labs directory, then move to it (you can use the cd or pushd command in Unix to move to a directory).
  2. Perform LAB 5.1 - 5.5 and LAB 5.6 Options 1 & 2.
  3. Use your editor to write the answers to the questions and exercises in Lab 4 in the file Lab4answers.txt locating it in directory lab4
