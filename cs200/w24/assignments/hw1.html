Homework Assignment 1: Let's Make a Deal

This assignment is an exercise in stochastic discrete event simulation demonstrating & computer modeling a seemingly paradoxical principle of probability as embodied in the Monty Hall problem which emerged from a mathematical analysis of the optimal strategy for winning the best prize on the television game show, Let's Make a Deal.

Conditional execution statements (introduced in Chapter 11 of Reed) will be required to perform this homework.

You will borrow and adapt code from slots.html in Figure 11.10 on page 207 of Reed,
and from your solution to Exercise 11.19 on pages 213–214.

      • the assignment & number
      • the date
    2. Centered at the top of the Webpage will be the same information that is in the title.
