Monte Carlo Darts

by David Reed, for A Balanced Introduction to Computer Science, 2nd ed.

This Web page generates random points within a square and reports the number of points that land inside the inscribed circle. Simply enter the desired number of points and click the button labeled "Click to generate the points". You may click the button multiple times to continue adding points and accumulating counts. If you wish to start with a new square, click on the button labeled "Clear points and reset counts" (clicking Reload in the browser will not reset the counts).
