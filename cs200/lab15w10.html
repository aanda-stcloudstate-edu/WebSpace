Lab 15

[A. A. Anda]
Spring 2010

Introduction:

This lab exercises the ability to:
  • construct and test hardware Boolean circuits, from circuit diagrams, using breadboard kits.

Lab Assignment:

Form into pairs (in the hardware lab), then select, complete, and test one (or more) of the following tasks:
  • demonstrate that one can emulate each of the AND, OR, and NOT gates using only the sole sufficient operators, NAND & NOR respectively.
    • 7402 NOR Quad 2-input gate diagram
    • 7400 NAND Quad 2-input gate diagram
  • construct a four-bit adder using four full adders based on this logic design which configures a full-adder from an OR gate, two AND gates and two XOR (exclusive OR) gates.
    • 7486 - Quad 2-Input Exclusive-OR (XOR) gate diagram
  • construct a SR flip-flop from Figures 16.17 & 16.18 in Reed
Demonstrate your correctly operating circuit to the instructor.
